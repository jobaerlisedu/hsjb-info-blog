Transmission Modes in Computer Networks

Jan 02, 2026 | Networking

Introduction

Transmission mode refers to the mechanism of transferring data between two devices. There are three types:

Types

Simplex Mode

Unidirectional. Only one device transmits. Example: Keyboard, TV.

Half-Duplex Mode

Bidirectional, but one at a time. Example: Walkie-talkie.

Full-Duplex Mode

Bidirectional and simultaneous. Example: Telephone.

Comparison

Mode Direction Performance
Simplex One-way Low
Half-Duplex Two-way (Alternate) Moderate
Full-Duplex Two-way (Simultaneous) High
